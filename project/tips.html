Study Tips

Small habits can help you improve your English every day.

Build a Daily Routine

Study for 10–20 minutes every day. Short and frequent practice is better than long sessions.

Use English Actively

Speak out loud, write simple sentences, and repeat what you hear to improve faster.

Review Vocabulary

Write new words in a notebook and review them regularly to remember them longer.

Choose Interesting Content

Watch videos, listen to music, or read topics you enjoy to stay motivated.
